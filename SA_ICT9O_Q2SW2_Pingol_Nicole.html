Cat Breeds

Image Breed Description
Scottish Fold
 The most recognizable feature about Scottish Folds are their ears which are folded down. Combined with their big eyes and unique expression, they tend to look like owls.
Birman
 Birman kittens develop their colors as they grow, but they are born all white. This type of cat's white paws serves as it's unique feature.
Burmilla
 This cat breed can have long or short hair. It has a muscular yet elegant body shape, and it's eyes are usually yellow or green.
British Shorthair
 As the name suggests, this cat usually has a short coat of fur. They are a medium to large type of cat and can have different colors or patterns.
Ragdoll
 These are semi-longhaired cats that usually have pretty blue eyes. These are gentle cats that mostly love being around humans.
Russian Blue
 This type of cat has silver-blue eyes, vibrant green eyes, and is shorthaired. This cat is the shy and sensitive type.
Siberian
 This is a longhaired, medium to large cat. They are strong cats with a calm facial expression, their eyes can come in different colors such as green, blue, and gold.
Persian
 This breed has many different kinds of variations such as Solid, Silver & Golden, Smoke & Shaded, Tabby, etc. They have thick coats and gentle personalities.
American Shorthair
 This cat has a pretty appearance and a nice personality, it comes in many different colors. It is safe to have around children or other pets.
Japanese Bobtail
 A feature you can immediatley recognize is the short tail this cat has. They are friendly and playful.
